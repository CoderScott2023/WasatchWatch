Our Mission

The Wasatch Front is changing, and many Utahns remain unaware of the environmental shifts happening in their own backyard. Our goal is to bring accessible, data-driven insights to all citizens, helping them understand how air quality, climate, and urban expansion are impacting their communities. By spreading this information, we empower individuals to take action and protect the future of the Wasatch Front.

We also seek to provide open-source, easily accessible, and free code so you can replicate and check our results. We use open-source, publicly available data in everything we do, so reproducing our conclusions is easy and accessible.
